# Product Brief

## Vision

Build an AI-powered equity intelligence platform that continuously gathers, analyzes, correlates, and summarizes stock-market information for retail investors.

The platform is not a trading advisor. It is an explainable intelligence system that transforms large volumes of market information into traceable insights.

## Markets

- Primary: India
- Reference: United States, Japan

Reference markets are monitored to detect global developments affecting Indian equities, including Federal Reserve decisions, tariffs, oil prices, currency movements, Japanese rates, and geopolitical events.

## Core Questions

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- Why does it matter?
- Who benefits?
- Who is negatively impacted?
- Is this confirmed or rumor?
- What should investors monitor next?
- What opportunities and risks are emerging?

## Dashboard Sections

- Market Pulse
- Top Opportunities
- Top Risks
- Global Impact
- Sector Trends
- Event Feed
- Company Explorer
- Conversational AI
- Daily Reports

## Intelligence Contract

Every conclusion should include:

- Sources
- Confidence
- Reasoning
- Impact explanation
- Watch points
